Semester Project

summary

As part of a semester project, we collected several 3D point clouds from various locations around the uni. In each case, we used
  1. a single camera with a SLAM package, and
  2. a lidar scanner.
Existing structural descriptors were tested to see whether they can be used for finding correspondences between the two types of point clouds.
